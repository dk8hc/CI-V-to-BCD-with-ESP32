# CI-V-to-BCD-with-ESP32
CI-V to BCD with ESP32
Should work with all CI-V Adresses,
If PTT connected it will block switching BCD at state pulled to GND

160m goes 1
80m goes 2
40m goes 3
30m goes 4
20m goes 5
17m goes 6
15m goes 7
12m goes 8
10m goes 9
6m goes 10
2m goes 11
70cm goes 12
4m and 60m goes 15, (some Yaesu devices goes 1111 at 60m some at 4m)
